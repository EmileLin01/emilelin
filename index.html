Solar Energy in
Mozambique

CPCS, a global infrastructure consulting firm, found a way around Mozambique's energy deficiency, which is to store solar energy in batteries. Eager to share its solution with the world, CPCS hired me to ghostwrite a thought leadership article detailing its energy procurement strategy. The article was later picked up by Energy Global, a leading magazine in electrical engineering, receiving wide readership.

This article speaks to my ability to ask the right questions to experts and to repackage their answers into a story that appeals to a broad audience.
